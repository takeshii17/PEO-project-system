# PEO-project-system


A Provincial Engineering Office (PEO) is a local government department responsible for planning, designing, constructing, and maintaining public infrastructure within a province. It oversees projects such as provincial roads, bridges, public buildings, drainage systems, and other government facilities.

The office ensures that infrastructure projects follow safety standards, engineering regulations, and budget guidelines. It also conducts inspections, prepares technical plans, supervises construction activities, and provides engineering support to other provincial departments to support community development and public safety.
